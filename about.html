About

Happy Medium is an application that simplifies deciding meeting places. Rooms are created where users share their current location, which determine the fairest options for places to meet. Our soon-to-be patented fairness algorithm is based on several factors: number of users, their vicinity, and commute distance.

Happy Medium takes the stress out of finding a meeting place everyone can agree on. Let us think for you, so you don't have to.
